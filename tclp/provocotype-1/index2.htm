David’s Clause

SME-Level Net-Zero Obligations

Plug-and-play clause for SMEs that imposes mutual net-zero commitments, requiring public targets, transition plans, annual reporting, employee training—and financial penalties (climate remediation fee) for non-compliance.

Miko’s Clause

Greener, Fairer Renewable Energy Sourcing

Obligates renewable energy supply-chain stakeholders to reduce carbon impact, ensure social and environmental safeguards (e.g., avoid modern slavery), measure and certify carbon footprints, and deliver due diligence and environmental assessment reporting.

Updated: 10 September 2024

Marni’s Clause

Climate-Risk in Reports on Title for Commercial Property

Enhances standard commercial-property title reports by prompting inclusion of climate-risk analysis—covering physical risks like flooding and transition risks impacting insurance, value, marketability, with recommendations for further searches, specialist advice, and stakeholder disclosures.

Updated: 2 July 2025
