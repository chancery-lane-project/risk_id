Borrower:TechGlobal Holdings Ltd(No. 06123456), 77 Innovation Park, Cambridge, CB4 0GF, United Kingdom(“Borrower”).

Business Day means a day(other than Saturday or Sunday)on which banks are open in London.

Drawdown Notice has the meaning in Schedule 1. Final Maturity Date means 31 August 2030. Finance Documents means this Agreement and any other document designated as such.

Interest Period means each period selected by the Borrower per clause 6. Loan means each advance made under the Facility.

Margin means 2.50% per annum over SONIA.

SONIA means the Sterling Overnight Index Average, as published by the Bank of England.

References to a person include bodies corporate, unincorporated associations and partnerships.

Headings do not affect interpretation; “including”means“including without limitation”.

The Lender makes available to the Borrower a revolving credit facility in an aggregate amount equal to the Commitment, for general corporate purposes.

Subject to the terms of this Agreement, Loans may be borrowed, repaid and re-borrowed until the Final Maturity Date.

Delivery of constitutional documents, board resolutions, specimen signatures and incumbency certificates.

Legal opinions satisfactory to the Lender.

Evidence of authority and compliance with Applicable Law.

The Borrower shall deliver a Drawdown Notice by 11:00 a.m.

three(3)Business Days prior to the proposed Drawdown Date, specifying the amount(minimum £1, 000, 000)and Interest Period.

Utilisation is subject to no Event of Default continuing on the proposed Drawdown Date and representations being true and correct.

Each Loan bears interest at the rate of SONIA plus the Margin.

Interest accrues daily and is payable on the last day of each Interest Period and on repayment.

Interest Periods shall be one, three or six months(as available), selected in the relevant Drawdown Notice.

Default interest:2% per annum above the rate otherwise applicable to any overdue amount.

Arrangement Fee.

0.50% flat on the Commitment, payable on Signing.

Commitment Fee.

0.25% per annum on the undrawn and uncancelled Commitment, payable quarterly in arrears.

The Borrower shall repay each Loan in full on the last day of its Interest Period unless such Loan is rolled over in accordance with this Agreement.

All outstanding Loans are repayable in full on the Final Maturity Date.

Voluntary prepayment is permitted with not less than five(5)Business Days’ written notice; prepayments shall be accompanied by accrued interest.

Mandatory prepayment upon change of control of the Borrower.

9. Taxes, Increased Costs and Illegality All payments shall be made free and clear of withholding taxes unless required by law.

If withholding is required, the Borrower shall gross up the payment(subject to customary exceptions).

If the Lender incurs increased costs due to changes in law or regulation, the Borrower shall, upon demand, indemnify the Lender for such additional costs.

If it becomes unlawful for the Lender to maintain the Facility, the Lender may cancel its Commitment and declare the Loans due and payable.

The Borrower is duly incorporated and validly existing; has power to own its assets and carry on its business; and has the power and authority to enter into and perform this Agreement.

This Agreement constitutes legal, valid and binding obligations of the Borrower.

No Default is continuing and no litigation is pending or threatened which could have a material adverse effect.

Deliver audited annual financial statements within 120 days of year-end and quarterly management accounts within 30 days of quarter-end.

Promptly notify the Lender of any Default.

Maintain all authorisations and comply with Applicable Law.

Negative pledge:not to create security over its assets(other than permitted security)without Lender consent.

Interest Cover Ratio ≥ 3.0:1(tested quarterly).

Net Debt to EBITDA ≤ 3.5:1(tested quarterly).

Each of the following is an Event of Default:Non-payment of any amount due under a Finance Document(subject to a two Business Day grace period for administrative error).

Breach of financial covenant or other undertaking not remedied within any applicable cure period.

Misrepresentation in any material respect.

Insolvency, insolvency proceedings or analogous events.

Cross-default:default under Financial Indebtedness exceeding £5, 000, 000. Unlawfulness or repudiation of a Finance Document.

On and at any time after an Event of Default, the Lender may cancel the Commitment, declare all or part of the Loans immediately due and payable, and/or enforce any rights.

The Borrower shall, within three(3)Business Days of demand, pay the Lender the amount of all reasonable costs and expenses(including legal fees)incurred in connection with the negotiation, preparation, execution, administration and enforcement of the Finance Documents.

The Parties shall keep Confidential Information confidential and use it solely for the purposes of this Agreement, subject to customary exceptions for disclosures required by law or regulators.

The Lender may assign or transfer its rights and obligations to another lender of equivalent standing subject to confidentiality undertakings.

The Borrower may not assign without Lender consent.

Amendments.

Must be in writing and signed by the Parties.

Governing Law and Jurisdiction.

England and Wales; courts of England have exclusive jurisdiction.
